Albert Einstein

Creator, scientist, inventor, and unmistakable

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein:

  • March 14, 1879 - Born in Ulm, in Württemberg, Germany
  • 1879 -The family moved to Munich, where he later on began his schooling at the Luitpold Gymnasium"
  • 1896 - Then entered the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to be trained as a teacher in physics and mathematics.
  • 1901 - The year he gained his diploma and acquired Swiss citizenship
  • 1905 - Obtained his doctor’s degree.
  • 1908 - Produced much of his remarkable work and was appointed Privatdozent in Berne.
  • 1909 - Became Professor Extraordinary at Zurich
  • 1911 - Becomes Professor of Theoretical Physics at Prague, returning to Zurich in the following year to fill a similar post.
  • 1914 - Appointed Director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Physical Institute and Professor in the University of Berlin
  • 1933 - Renounced his citizenship for political reasons and emigrated to America to take the position of Professor of Theoretical Physics at Princeton.
  • 1905 - Special Theory of Relativity.
  • 1916 - General Theory of Relativity.
  • 1926 - Investigations on Theory of Brownian Movement.
  • 1938 - The Evolution of Physics.
  • 1930 - Among his non-scientific works, About Zionism.
  • 1933 - Why war?
  • 1934 - My Philosophy.
  • 1950 - Out of My Later Years.
  • 1925 and 1935 - He gained numerous awards in recognition of his work, including the Copley Medal of the Royal Society of London, and the Franklin Medal of the Franklin Institute.
  • 1947 - states "I saw how excessive nationalism can spread like a disease, bringing tragedy to millions.
  • 1955 - He died on April 18, at Princeton, New Jersey..
